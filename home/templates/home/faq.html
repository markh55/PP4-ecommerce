Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Here are some of the most common questions we receive from our clients. Ask us directly if you still have questions!

1. What's the difference between web design and web development?

Web design focuses on the visual aspects and user experience of your website, while web development involves building the functionality and coding behind it. Both work together to create a complete website.

2. How much does a typical website cost?

The cost depends on the package you choose. You can view our packages and pricing here.

3. How long does it take to build a website?

Project timelines vary depending on the package and scope of work. We will provide an estimated timeline when you select a package and submit your project details.

4. Do you specialize in websites for small businesses?

Yes, we work with clients of all sizes, including small businesses, to create professional and effective websites.

5. Will my website work on mobile devices?

Absolutely. All our websites are built to be fully responsive and look great on desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

6. Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes, we offer ongoing maintenance and support packages to ensure your website stays up-to-date and secure.

7. Will my website be optimized for search engines?

All websites are built with basic SEO best practices in mind. For more advanced SEO, we can discuss additional services.

8. Can you redesign my existing website?

Yes, we can redesign your website to improve its look, functionality, and performance. Contact us to discuss your current website and goals.

Still have questions? We'd love to help!
